I work where quantum topology meets applied quantum computing. My doctoral research centered on the volume conjecture — hyperbolic geometry, topological quantum field theories, and skein-theoretic approaches to quantum invariants.

Beyond pure topology I build with quantum algorithms, error correction, ZX-calculus, and machine learning, with a continuing pull toward topological phases of matter, topological quantum computing, and topological data analysis.
